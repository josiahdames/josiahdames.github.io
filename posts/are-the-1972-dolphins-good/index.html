Are the 1972 Dolphins just as good as other historically great teams?

NFL
Author

Josiah Dames

Published

December 8, 2024

The 1972 Dolphins are the only team in NFL history to go undefeated and win the Super Bowl the same year. Led by quarterback Bob Griese and head coach Don Shula, the Dolphins did the impossible and no one has even been close to breaking that record, with the exception of the 2007 New England Patriots.

While the ’72 Dolphins were regarded as one of the best teams in NFL history, that doesn’t always mean that they were the “best” team statistically. There are some teams, like the 1985 Chicago Bears, who didn’t have a great offense but were carried by the defense.

If the ’72 Dolphins were that good, how do they compared with other great teams in NFL history? The league has changed a lot in the last 50 years. But before we answer that question, what about the other ’72 teams?

The Pro Football Reference’s “Team Offense” statistics show each seasons’s offense, including yards, completions, yards per play, and points.

To determine if the ’72 Dolphins were a great team, we must look at if they are even the best team that year statistically. (Talk about what information you’re putting into the graphs).
